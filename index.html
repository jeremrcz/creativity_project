Few steps to make an awesome raclette

Step 1: Buy the potatoes, the cheese and the charcuterie

Step 2: Find a raclette apparel

Step 3: Boil the potatoes and cut the cheese

Step 4: Start the raclette apparel

Step 5: Tell your friends to sit down at the table

Step 6: Put the cheese in the apparel for around 5 min

Step 7: Put the melted cheese on the potatoe

Step 8: Enjoy your evening with your friends or family
